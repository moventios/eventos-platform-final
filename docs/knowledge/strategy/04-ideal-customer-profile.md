# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**The Platform (EKB)**

## Primary ICP

**Mid-to-Large B2B Organizations with Hybrid Physical-Digital Operations**

- **Size:** 50-5000+ employees, multiple locations or high-volume events/spaces.
- **Industries:** Venues, event organizers, logistics hubs, universities/campuses, corporate facilities, sports/entertainment, warehouses with value-added services.
- **Pain:** Double bookings, financial discrepancies, audit failures, manual reconciliations, lack of real-time visibility, fear of AI errors in critical paths.
- **Current Tools:** Mix of spreadsheets, legacy booking software, separate accounting, basic ticketing.
- **Decision Makers:** COO, Head of Operations, CFO (for finance), CIO/CTO (for integration/sovereignty).
- **Users:** Operations team, finance, event managers, facility staff, IT admins.

**Why They Buy:**
- Need one system that handles space + commerce + money + workflow with proof.
- Value tenant-like control even internally (multiple depts).
- Want to avoid vendor lock-in for long-term ops.
- See AI as opportunity but need guardrails.

## Secondary ICP

- **Implementation Partners / System Integrators:** Who implement for end customers.
- **Enterprise IT Teams:** Building internal platforms on top.
- **Emerging:** Companies in regulated physical ops (healthcare facilities, gov venues).

## Expansion ICP

- Smaller operators via partners or self-serve (once UX simplified).
- International with multi-currency/multi-jurisdiction needs (leverage immutable ledger).

## Buying Triggers

- Audit failure or compliance issue.
- Scaling events/spaces leading to conflicts.
- Digital transformation initiative.
- Replacing legacy or multiple tools.
- AI pilot that needs governance.

## Churn Risks (Anti-ICP)

- Pure digital companies (no physical operations).
- Very small teams happy with spreadsheets.
- Those wanting "AI magic" without human oversight.
