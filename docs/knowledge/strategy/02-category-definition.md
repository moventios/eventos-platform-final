# Category Definition

**Platform Enterprise Knowledge Base (EKB) - Neutral Name**

## Recommended Category

**Moventios**

Movent = Reusable Relationship Infrastructure.
Moventios = the first public Relationship, Activation & Collaboration Network built on Movent.
Vertical Solutions (Events, Projects, Communities, etc.) are built on top of the same Movent engines.

### Why This Category?

From benchmark research (ERP, CRM, Workflow, Event, Booking, Hospitality, Logistics, AI Native):

- ERP (SAP, Oracle): Too broad, heavy, not specialized for physical operations + real-time spatial.

- Event (Eventbrite, Cvent): Focus on ticketing/events, no deep financial ledger or facility management.

- Booking/Facility (Skedda, Robin): Good for space booking, but no commerce, finance, AI.

- Hospitality (Mews, Opera): Strong for hotels, but not general physical logistics or AI RAG.

- AI Native (Glean, Linear): AI first, but not operations backbone with immutable finance.

This platform focuses on helping organizations plan, coordinate, execute, monitor, and audit operations involving events, venues, projects, teams, logistics, schedules, resources, assets, vendors, finance, payments, approvals, and documentation.

Core capabilities (scheduling, resources, finance, people, approvals, execution) are supported by strong underlying implementation (financial integrity, AI assistance, operational intelligence, developer extensibility). The customer buys the operational solution.

### Comparison to Existing

- Not ERP: No full HR, manufacturing, etc.
- Not pure Event or Booking: Deeper finance, AI, governance.
- Unique: Immutable ledger + AI safety + tenant sovereignty + Stoic UX for pros.

**Positioning Frame:**
For organizations that run events, projects, venues, and related operations, the Platform provides the coordination, execution, visibility, accountability, and auditability needed to reduce chaos and increase reliability.

**Evidence from the domain:**
The core work described is planning and running events and projects: resources, schedules, teams, logistics, finance, approvals, and documentation.

Supporting capabilities (financial systems, AI, intelligence) exist to make those operations more reliable and auditable.

**Final Recommendation:** Moventios.
