# Brand Audit

**Date:** 2026-06-25

## Current State

The project has no formal brand strategy, positioning, or identity beyond engineering principles in Layer 1 (Stoic Ledger, Quiet Power, AI-First, Zero Trust, Tenant Sovereignty, Immutable, Open Standards).

Current product identity is implicit through focus on operational reliability, coordination, and auditability for events and projects.

## Analysis from References

- brand.md style: Excellent foundation for AI brand context.
- Brand-building-skills: Modular approach for brand work.
- openmaster: Architecture example.

## Gaps

- No brand architecture.
- Product positioning has drifted toward generic "enterprise OS" language.
- No clear, consistent external category (Moventios).
- Messaging in some documents still emphasizes internal architecture and philosophy over customer operational benefits.

## Recommendations

- Re-anchor all public/strategy documents to "Moventios".
- Keep internal engineering language (Layers, sovereignty as technical principle, immutability, etc.) in `docs/layers/` and architecture docs.
- Define external brand elements focused on easier operations, reliability, coordination, visibility, and auditability.
