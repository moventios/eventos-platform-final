# Competitive Landscape & Benchmark Matrix

**The Platform (EKB)**

## Category Context

We compete in the intersection of ERP, Workflow, Event/Booking, Facility Management, and emerging AI Operations platforms.

## Key Competitors (Summarized from Research)

**ERP (SAP, Oracle, Dynamics, Odoo):**
- Strengths: Broad, financials strong.
- Weaknesses: Heavy, not specialized for physical/spatial real-time, high customization cost.
- Our Edge: Focused coordination for events/projects with built-in reliability and auditability.

**Event (Eventbrite, Cvent):**
- Strengths: Ticketing, registration.
- Weaknesses: Weak finance, no deep facility or ledger.
- Our Edge: Coordinated operations with strong finance, visibility, and auditability for events and projects.

**Booking/Facility (Skedda, Robin):**
- Strengths: Space management.
- Weaknesses: Limited commerce/finance/AI.
- Our Edge: Integrated coordination, finance, intelligence, and extensibility.

**Hospitality (Mews, Opera):**
- Strengths: PMS for hotels.
- Weaknesses: Vertical specific, not general logistics/events.
- Our Edge: More general for any physical ops + strong AI/governance.

**AI Native (Glean, Linear):**
- Strengths: AI search/productivity.
- Weaknesses: Not operations or finance backbone.
- Our Edge: AI under strict control + full ops system.

**Workflow (ServiceNow):**
- Strengths: Enterprise workflows.
- Weaknesses: Expensive, not physical spatial or immutable finance.
- Our Edge: Purpose-built for hybrid with financial truth.
