# Value Proposition Canvas

**Event & Project Operations Platform**

## Customer Profile

**Customer Jobs:**
- Plan and run events and projects without coordination chaos.
- Allocate resources, manage schedules, and track progress across teams.
- Handle finance, payments, approvals, and documentation reliably.
- Maintain visibility and accountability.
- Get useful intelligence without losing control.

**Pains:**
- Coordination chaos across teams, schedules, and resources.
- Fragmented tools and manual work.
- Lack of real-time visibility and accountability.
- Expensive or weak auditability.
- Tools that don't fit how events and projects actually run.

**Gains:**
- Clear visibility into status and issues.
- Reduced manual chasing and dropped tasks.
- Stronger accountability and easier audits.
- More reliable execution at scale.
- Useful assistance (including AI) that fits the workflow.

## Value Map

**Products & Services:**
- Coordinated system for scheduling, resources, teams, finance, approvals, execution, and documentation for events and projects.
- Supporting capabilities for reliable financial handling, operational intelligence, and safe AI assistance.
- Extensibility and integrations.

**Pain Relievers:**
- Single place for coordination instead of fragmented tools.
- Built-in visibility, approvals, and records reduce manual work and risk.
- Strong auditability for operations and finance.
- AI assistance that is controlled and useful.

**Gain Creators:**
- Better on-time, on-budget execution.
- Clear accountability across the operation.
- Easier compliance and post-event/project reviews.
- Extensible foundation that grows with the organization.

## Fit

The Platform relieves the pains of coordination chaos and weak visibility/accountability while creating gains in reliable execution and auditability for events and projects.

This is derived from the core operational domain. Will be refined with customer input.
